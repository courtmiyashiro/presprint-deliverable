Team Project Proposal

Prepared by: Madison Solutions (Group 12)

Project Overview

Madison Solutions will utilize both CIS and SMAD resources to deliver a functional Management Information System for our client, the Ole School Alumni Scholarship Group. This organization is responsible for awarding scholarships and offering a mentorship program to students to assist them with internship and job opportunities. One of their primary responsibilities is facilitating communication between their corporate sponsors and students. Therefore, they require a centralized system where students and members can easily navigate through OSASG information and resources.

The fully developed system will be hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS). It will track data necessary to the client on Microsoft SQL Server and include a user interface designed using Microsoft Visual Studio.

Project Goals

  • Develop an intuitive and fully functional website and database
  • Communicate effectively and professionally with all stakeholders to produce the best outcome
  • Meet and exceed our client's expectations
  • Improve user satisfaction by ensuring our application runs smoothly

Timeline

Our overall timeline is based on the set sprint due dates. These dates include March 24, April 5, and April 19. Madison Solutions has our own deadlines set for each deliverable to be completed before the sprint presentations. We plan to have all deliverables completed three days before the official sprint due dates. This will allow our team to make any necessary changes before the sprint due dates. As the project progresses, we will create more specific deadlines within each sprint based on the amount of work each team member is assigned.
